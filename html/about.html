About Anna Allen

Education

After many long and grueling years studying at Stockport Grammar School, Stockport, Anna went on to spend 3 years studying Zoology at The University of Liverpool in Liverpool. Here she gained her degree in Zoology before heading to Canada for a year to recover.

Inspiration

Anna's love for photography grew whilst she was in Canada, with so much amazing wildlife and breathtaking scenery to snap, she soon became a dab hand with a camera and the idea for our Photography Competition was born.

Photo Comp

Established in 2007 the Photo Comp aims to help amateur photographers become recognised within the photography community. The winning photographer from each category will receive a contract with an esteemed establishment to help kick start their professional photography career
